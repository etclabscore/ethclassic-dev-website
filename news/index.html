Dear Ethereum Classic stakeholder,

The Ethereum Classic network successfully executed a hard fork at block 8,772,000 mil on September 12, 2019. Major exchanges, wallets, dapps, and services have updated their clients to Atlantis-enabled versions. Ensure your GethClassic, MultiGeth, and/ or Parity clients are updated.

  • Classic-Geth, version 6.0.9-stable or later.
  • Multi-Geth, version 1.9.2-stable or later.
  • Parity, version 2.5.6-stable or later.
